# **Parent vs Aggregator**

|**Parent**|**Aggregator**|
|--|--|
|- **a POM which its content can be inherited** <br> - **1 project/child POM can only have 1 Parent** <br> - **child declare parantage in its POM** <br> - **Parent is unaware of child that's inheriting** <br> - **useful when parent POM consist of reusable content for another project** |- **can sequence build process of many projects** <br> - **specifies all the projects that can be build-managed together** <br> - **child POM unaware of aggregator POM** <br> - **Aggregator POM can have more than 1 child POM** <br> - **no inheritance of any content from aggregator POM** <br> - **useful for modular build & site those projects that should be managed together** |
